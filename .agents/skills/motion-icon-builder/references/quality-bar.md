# Icon quality bar

Technical validity is necessary and insufficient. Score only after viewing the
static mark and loop without its label or concept. The reviewer must name the
closest precedent and the strongest reason not to ship.

## Scored review

| Dimension | Weight | Evidence required |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Static form and small-size legibility | 25 | Silhouette, negative space, stroke weight, and 13/16px rendering |
| Originality and authorship | 20 | Distance from each reference and absence of preset-like construction |
| Semantic clarity | 15 | What the still denotes and what action an unlabeled loop communicates |
| Choreography and physical logic | 15 | Primary event, pivot/path, weight, attachment, interruption, and reverse |
| Optical refinement across frames | 15 | Visual centering, overshoot, edge clearance, and absence of shimmer |
| Restraint and family cohesion | 10 | Necessity of every moving part and fit with the collection's character |

Passing requires at least 92/100, no dimension below 80%, written evidence for
every deduction, an independent reviewer `PASS`, and explicit user lock.

## Automatic KILL conditions

- A distinctive silhouette or path construction is attributable to one source.
- Recognition depends on animation or rationale text.
- Motion is decorative, preset-like, or semantically unnecessary.
- Any delayed or staggered part finishes after the 300ms total budget.
- The mark is illegible at 13px or 16px, changes apparent stroke weight, or
  shimmers during motion.
- Optical imbalance is unexplained or a peak frame crowds/clips the viewBox.
- The result is merely competent. A clean `npm run check` cannot overrule this.

`REVISE` must list exact changes and be reviewed again. `KILL` ends that
direction. Zero surviving candidates is a successful review outcome.
